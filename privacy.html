Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

OldPhotoFixer is a privacy-first photo restoration tool. Your photos are processed entirely within your web browser using artificial intelligence, and never leave your device. We do not collect, store, upload, or transmit your photos to any server.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data to operate our website:

  • No photos: Your photos are processed locally in your browser and never sent to our servers.
  • No personal information: We do not require registration, email, or any personal information to use our tool.
  • No cookies: We do not use tracking cookies or any similar technologies.
  • No analytics: We do not use analytics services that track your browsing behavior.

How We Process Photos

When you use OldPhotoFixer:

  1. Your photo is loaded directly into your browser's memory
  2. Our AI model runs entirely within your browser to process the image
  3. The processed result is displayed on your screen
  4. You can download the result directly to your device

At no point does your photo data leave your device.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • ONNX Runtime Web: An open-source AI engine that runs in your browser to process images
  • Google Fonts: For typography on our website
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider for the website itself (static files only)

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
